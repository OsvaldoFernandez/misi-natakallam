NT connects refugees/displaced persons to remote work opportunities in the language sector.

The Syrian conflict has triggered the worst refugee crisis of our time, and millions of displaced persons struggle to access an income. NT connects them to language tutoring and translation services online.
Looking to practice a language? We’ll find your most compatible partner.
Need something translated? Get it done while providing livelihoods to displaced persons, worldwide.
Are you part of a company, school, NGO or university? Sign up to NT for your institution!

Impact
