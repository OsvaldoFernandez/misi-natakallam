Translation & Interpretation services

NaTakallam provides translation & transcription services delivered by refugees from Syria, Iraq, Afghanistan, Pakistan, Iran, & the DRC.

Translation services have primarily been from English to: Arabic, French, Kurdish, Urdu, Farsi, Pashto & Dari. Our translators also translate to English from their native language & from French to Arabic, Kurdish or Farsi.

Interpretation services can be done over Skype or in-person, based on location.

Cost

All rates are pending difficulty, management time, & turnaround rate. For interpretation, it will also be subject to location and detailed needs.

Price is always agreed upon per a written agreement between client & NaTakallam.
