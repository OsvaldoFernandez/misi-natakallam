Learn a language, change a life. Since starting in 2015, NaTakallam has paired over 100 displaced persons and over 2,500 individuals in more than 65 countries who have engaged in more than 20,000 hours of NaTakallam sessions. Displaced people have self-generated over $300,000 through their work with NaTakallam.

Part of NaTakallam's success comes from pairing individuals not just with Arabic teachers, but with conversation partners that will share their experiences and interests as well as their language. Be part of the conversation in three easy steps:

Step 3: meet your partner

Be on the lookout for an email from NaTakallam! We'll introduce you to your partner and let you take it from there.
